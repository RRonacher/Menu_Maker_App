Test Family Chili

Ingredients
  • • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • • 1 onion, diced
  • • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • • 400 g ground beef
  • • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • • 1 can kidney beans, drained
  • • Salt to taste
Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add minced garlic and cook 1 minute more.

2. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon. Drain excess fat.

3. Stir in diced tomatoes, chili powder, and kidney beans. Bring to a simmer.

4. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season with salt to taste.

5. Serve hot with your choice of toppings.
